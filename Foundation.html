Introduction to the Foundation

Established in 2020 under the visionary guidance of Sahabzada Muhammad Mounis Murad Naushahi and his siblings, the Naushahi Foundation stands as a beacon of compassion. Rooted in the spiritual legacy of the Naushahi order, we are dedicated to serving humanity with dignity, integrity, and unwavering commitment.

Our Mission

To embody the spirit of selfless service by uplifting the underserved, providing essential sustenance, and fostering a community built on empathy and mutual support. We believe that true worship is found in the service of God's creation.

Core Values

Compassion (Khidmat)

Serving with humility and grace, recognizing the divine spark in every individual.

Integrity (Amanat)

Upholding the highest standards of trust, transparency, and ethical responsibility.

Community (Ittihad)

Strengthening the social fabric through unity, cooperation, and shared welfare.

Dignity (Izzat)

Respecting the sanctity and honor of every human life we are privileged to serve.

Founder's Vision

"The path to spiritual elevation lies in the service of humanity." Guided by this profound truth and the legacy of Allama Mian Noor Muhammad Nusrat Naushahi, our foundation strives to be a source of relief for the weary and hope for the destitute.

From our inception, we have been steadfast in our commitment to hunger relief, educational empowerment, and social welfare. We envision a society where compassion is not just an act, but a way of life, creating lasting positive change across Pakistan.
