About Us

Our Foundation and Purpose

Introduction

Awan-e-Faqr-e-Naushahia is a Pakistan-based religious and charitable organization committed to serving communities through faith-based initiatives, education, and social welfare programs. We strive to create positive change by fostering spiritual growth, moral development, and community harmony.

Founded with a deep commitment to Islamic principles and values, our organization has been working tirelessly to uplift communities across Pakistan. We believe in the power of faith, education, and compassionate service to transform lives and build stronger, more harmonious communities.

Historical Background

Our organization draws its spiritual foundation from the rich tradition of Islamic scholarship and Sufi teachings. The name "Awan-e-Faqr-e-Naushahia" reflects our commitment to the path of spiritual poverty (faqr) and service, following the noble teachings that emphasize humility, devotion, and service to humanity.

Over the years, we have established centers across Pakistan, from major cities to rural areas, ensuring that our services reach those who need them most. Our work is guided by the principles of compassion, justice, and the betterment of society.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of faith, knowledge, and compassion, inspiring individuals and communities across Pakistan to live with integrity, wisdom, and mutual respect. We envision a Pakistan where communities are strengthened through spiritual growth, education, and mutual support, creating an environment of peace, harmony, and prosperity for all.

Our Mission

To provide religious guidance, quality education, and essential support services that empower communities, strengthen families, and promote peace and understanding throughout Pakistan. We are committed to:

  • Providing authentic religious teachings and spiritual guidance
  • Offering comprehensive educational programs that combine religious knowledge with moral development
  • Supporting communities through welfare programs and social services
  • Promoting peace, tolerance, and interfaith harmony
  • Empowering youth through education and leadership development
  • Extending our services to both urban and rural areas across Pakistan

Core Values

Faith & Devotion

Rooted in spiritual principles and Islamic teachings, we approach all our work with sincerity, devotion, and a deep commitment to serving Allah through service to humanity.

Education & Knowledge

We believe in the transformative power of education. Through learning and moral training, we empower individuals to make positive contributions to their communities and society at large.

Community Service

Dedicated to uplifting those in need, we provide support, assistance, and care to individuals and families facing challenges, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Peace & Harmony

We actively work to build bridges between communities, promote understanding, and foster an environment of peace, tolerance, and mutual respect throughout Pakistan.

Integrity & Transparency

We conduct all our activities with the highest standards of integrity, transparency, and accountability, ensuring that resources are used effectively and responsibly.

Continuous Growth

We are committed to continuous improvement, learning, and growth, always seeking better ways to serve our communities and fulfill our mission.

Our Commitment

At Awan-e-Faqr-e-Naushahia, we are committed to serving the people of Pakistan with dedication, compassion, and excellence. We believe that by working together, we can create a brighter future for all, built on the foundations of faith, knowledge, and service.

We welcome all who share our values and wish to contribute to this noble mission. Whether through volunteering, donations, or participation in our programs, your support helps us continue this important work.
