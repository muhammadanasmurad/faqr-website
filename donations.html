Donations & Support

Your Generosity Makes a Difference

Your contributions enable us to continue our vital work across Pakistan. Every donation, regardless of size, helps us serve communities, support education, and provide essential services to those in need.

We ensure that all donations are used transparently and effectively, with the highest standards of accountability. Your support directly impacts the lives of individuals and families across Pakistan.

Zakat

Fulfill your Zakat obligation by supporting our programs that directly assist those eligible to receive Zakat according to Islamic principles. We ensure that Zakat funds are distributed to eligible recipients in accordance with Islamic guidelines.

  • Support for eligible Zakat recipients
  • Transparent distribution process
  • Compliance with Islamic principles

Sadaqah

Give voluntary charity to support our ongoing community welfare, education, and peacebuilding initiatives throughout Pakistan. Sadaqah can be given at any time and in any amount.

  • Support community welfare programs

  • Contribute to peacebuilding efforts
