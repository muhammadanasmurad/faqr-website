Allama Mian Noor Muhammad Nusrat Naushahi

Founder and Spiritual Guide of Awan-e-Faqr-e-Naushahia

Founder Biography

Allama Mian Noor Muhammad Nusrat Naushahi, the esteemed founder and spiritual guide of Awan-e-Faqr-e-Naushahia, dedicated his life to the pursuit of knowledge, spiritual enlightenment, and service to humanity.

A distinguished scholar, Allama Mian Noor Muhammad Nusrat Naushahi achieved the honor of being a Gold Medalist in MA Persian, demonstrating exceptional academic excellence and deep appreciation for classical literature and Islamic scholarship.

His educational journey encompassed extensive studies in spiritual sciences, herbal knowledge, and formal academic disciplines, reflecting his holistic approach to learning and understanding. This comprehensive knowledge base enabled him to guide others not only in matters of faith and spirituality but also in practical wisdom and traditional healing practices.

As the founder and spiritual guide of Awan-e-Faqr-e-Naushahia, he established an organization rooted in Islamic principles, dedicated to serving communities across Pakistan through religious guidance, education, and compassionate service. His vision was to create an institution that would continue to uplift communities, strengthen families, and promote peace and understanding.

Throughout his life, Allama Mian Noor Muhammad Nusrat Naushahi demonstrated unwavering dedication to knowledge, guidance, and community service. His legacy continues to inspire the work of Awan-e-Faqr-e-Naushahia, guiding the organization in its mission to serve faith, strengthen communities, and promote peace throughout Pakistan.

Co-Founder

Shahabzada Mian Muhammad Sohaib Suchyar Naushahi

Co-Founder

Shahabzada Mian Muhammad Sohaib Suchyar Naushahi, the son of the founder, has been an integral part of Awan-e-Faqr-e-Naushahia's mission and growth. His contributions have been instrumental in shaping the organization's educational and welfare initiatives.

Educated in herbal sciences and holding a BA degree, Shahabzada Mian Muhammad Sohaib Suchyar Naushahi brought a unique combination of traditional knowledge and formal education to the organization. His expertise in herbal sciences reflects the organization's commitment to preserving and sharing traditional wisdom alongside modern learning.

As a retired Government Teacher, he brought years of experience in education and pedagogy to the organization's educational programs. His dedication to teaching and learning has helped establish and maintain high standards in the educational initiatives of Awan-e-Faqr-e-Naushahia.

Throughout his career, Shahabzada Mian Muhammad Sohaib Suchyar Naushahi has actively supported educational and welfare initiatives, working tirelessly to ensure that the organization's programs reach those who need them most. His commitment to service and education continues to guide the organization's work in communities across Pakistan.

Descendants & Legacy

Sahabzada Muhammad Mounis Murad Naushahi

Founder of Naushahi Foundation

Muhammad Mounis Murad Naushahi represents the continuing legacy of service and dedication established by his forebears. Currently pursuing an MS in International Business Management (IBM) at the University of Bradford, he combines academic excellence with a deep commitment to humanitarian service.

As the founder of the Naushahi Foundation, Muhammad Mounis Murad Naushahi has established a non-governmental organization focused on humanitarian support. The foundation provides essential services including food and clothing to underprivileged communities, reflecting the values of compassion, service, and community support that have been central to the family's legacy.

His work with the Naushahi Foundation demonstrates how the principles and values of Awan-e-Faqr-e-Naushahia continue to inspire new generations to serve their communities and make a positive impact in the lives of those in need.
