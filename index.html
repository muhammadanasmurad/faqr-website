Welcome to Awan-e-Faqr-e-Naushahia

Awan-e-Faqr-e-Naushahia is a Pakistan-based religious and charitable organization committed to serving communities through faith-based initiatives, education, and social welfare programs. We strive to create positive change by fostering spiritual growth, moral development, and community harmony across Pakistan.

Our Mission

To provide religious guidance, quality education, and essential support services that empower communities, strengthen families, and promote peace and understanding throughout Pakistan.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of faith, knowledge, and compassion, inspiring individuals and communities across Pakistan to live with integrity, wisdom, and mutual respect.
